Welcome to the Chicago Curling Club

Founded in 1948, the Chicago Curling Club welcomes all visiters and members. Our facility has four sheets of ice, locker rooms, a fully-equipped kitchen, dining area, and a large social and viewing area. As with all sports, it's the players that make the game, and the members of the Chicago Curling Club have created a warm and welcoming place where you can enjoy a competitive sport and make and expand friendships. Scroll around the site and reach out to us to try it out. You'll be hooked just like we are.

Interested in curling?

Curling is an amazing sport! We'd love to share our passion with you. We offer a few ways to experince curling. The best way is to try one of our Learn 2 Curl programs. No prior experience is required. If you are looking for a great sport that combines friendly competition and fun times with others, curling is for you.

Looking for a bonspiel?

Bonspiels are a great way to see other clubs and socialize with new curlers. Our public bonspiels, available to all curlers, include the Windy City Open, Men's International, Heathers Gloamin' Gaels, The Old Boys, and the biennial Mixed Bonspiel. We also hold several events for our members, including the year-ending Auld Lange Syne. Our club also hosts and participates in regional MCA social bonspiels and playdown competitions.
